Work-From-Home / Teleworking Agreement

This Work-From-Home / Teleworking Agreement (the “Teleworking Agreement”), effective March 23, 2020 is between you (“Associate”) and Zensar Technologies, Ltd. or its applicable affiliate which employs you (“Employer”). This Teleworking Agreement shall be entered into by all Associates working remotely during the Covid-19 pandemic and shall remain in full force and effect until Employer directs Associate to return to Associate’s working location. This Teleworking Agreement may be revoked at any time by Employer, at Employer’s sole discretion.

1. Associate’s Obligations and Responsibilities When Working Remotely

a. Associate shall not use any of his or her personal devices to connect to any client network, ODC environment, etc. Associate will work only with Employer-provided or Client-provided equipment.

b. Associate shall provide a secure location for Employer- or Client-provided equipment and materials, and will not use, or allow others to use, such equipment for any purpose other than conducting Employer-related business. All equipment, records, documents, and information provided by Employer shall remain the property of Employer.

c. Associate shall implement reasonable information security practices at Associate’s remote working location, as well as any procedures specified by Employer or Employer’s Client(s). At a minimum, Associate shall work in a private area that will prevent unauthorized access to Zensar or Client information (i.e. shoulder-surfing or phone-eavesdropping) and shall maintain clear-screen and clear-desk practices. Associate agrees to seek the guidance of Associate’s supervisor if Associate believes Associate is unable to comply with these obligations.

d. Associate shall log off from all Client systems and applications immediately after each shift.

e. Associate shall lock Associate’s workstation at any time when away from Associate’s workspace.

f. Associate shall not screen-capture, copy/paste, print, save on a storage device (i.e. a local/network hard drive, USD, DVD, etc.) any data or information from any Employer- or Client-provided equipment.

g. Associate understands and agrees that all applicable Employer and Client policies, including information security and data privacy policies, remain in full force and effect when working remotely and shall not violate any such policy(ies).

2. General Terms

a. Employer may terminate this Teleworking Agreement at any time, with or without cause, immediately upon written or electronic notice. Employer will not be held responsible for costs, damages or losses resulting from stopping remote working.

b. This Teleworking Agreement is not a contract of employment or intended to offer a new employee benefit and may not be construed as such.

c. Associate understands and agrees that this Teleworking Agreement does not otherwise alter the duties, obligations, responsibilities, and terms of Associate’s employment with Employer and any of Employer’s Client(s).

d. The terms hereof supplement any existing work from home policy and should be read to further Employer’s organizational goals which are to enhance associate safety during the Covid-19 pandemic while delivering high-quality and continuous service for internal and external stakeholders and clients.

I have read and understood this Teleworking Agreement and accept its conditions. This Teleworking Agreement is without prejudice to any of Employer’s rights, remedies and defenses in your underlying Employment Agreement with Employer, now existing or hereafter arising, all of which are hereby expressly reserved.
